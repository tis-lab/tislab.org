Dr. Richmond is a Research Associate Professor in the Department of Genetics at the UNC School of Medicine. His research focuses on the integration of phenotypic and multi-omic data for the diagnosis of rare genetic disease. He is working within the TISlab to develop novel AI-driven approaches for phenotype recognition in patients to more rapidly identify those in need of genomic diagnostic testing, and expanding the capabilities of achieving a diagnosis with a multi-omic toolkit. 

### Education

- PhD Bioinformatics, University of British Columbia, 2020
