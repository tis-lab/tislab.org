Dr. Presskreischer is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Psychiatry working with the Suicide Prevention Institute, Center of Excellence in Eating Disorders, and the Translational and Integrative Sciences Lab. Her research interests include population mental health; local, state, and federal mental health policy; and health insurance coverage of mental health conditions.

### Education
- BA Individualized Study, New York University 2010
- MS Social Work, Columbia University 2015
- PhD Health and Public Policy, Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health 2021
